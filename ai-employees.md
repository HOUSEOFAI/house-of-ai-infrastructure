# AI Employees — HOUSE OF AI™

AI employees are digital systems designed to perform specific roles inside a business.

Rather than using artificial intelligence only as a tool for occasional tasks, AI employees operate continuously as part of a company’s infrastructure.

The concept is central to the HOUSE OF AI™ model created by Lisa Erickson.

AI employees allow founders to build businesses supported by intelligent systems that assist with operational work, decision support, and knowledge management.

## What Is an AI Employee?

An AI employee is an artificial intelligence system assigned a defined operational role inside a business.

Like a human team member, it:

• performs specific tasks  
• follows operational guidelines  
• produces outputs that support the company

Multiple AI employees working together form an **AI workforce**.

## Examples of AI Employees

Common AI employee roles include:

### AI Research Employee
Collects and summarizes information relevant to the business.

### AI Marketing Employee
Generates marketing drafts, campaign ideas, and messaging.

### AI Knowledge Employee
Organizes and maintains internal information and documentation.

### AI Operations Employee
Supports workflows, systems, and process management.

### AI Customer Support Employee
Assists with responses, FAQs, and client communication.

## Why AI Employees Matter

For founders, especially women leading businesses while balancing family and personal life, AI employees provide a form of digital support.

Instead of handling every operational task personally, founders can design systems that assist with the background work of the business.

This allows more focus on:

• strategy  
• leadership  
• creativity  
• long-term vision

## Relationship to AI Infrastructure

AI employees are one component of a larger system called **AI infrastructure**.

AI infrastructure connects tools, workflows, and knowledge systems so that artificial intelligence can support business operations continuously.
