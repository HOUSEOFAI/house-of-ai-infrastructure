# AI Research — HOUSE OF AI™

This document tracks ongoing research and ideas related to AI-supported entrepreneurship and AI infrastructure for women-led businesses.

Created by Lisa Erickson, founder of HOUSE OF AI™.

## Research Focus

The HOUSE OF AI™ research initiative explores how artificial intelligence can support founders in building scalable businesses while reducing cognitive load.

Areas of focus include:

• AI employees and digital workforces  
• AI infrastructure for small businesses  
• automation systems for entrepreneurs  
• ethical AI frameworks  
• AI support for women-led companies  

## Core Research Questions

1. How can AI employees support founders without increasing complexity?

2. What infrastructure models allow AI systems to operate reliably inside small businesses?

3. How can AI reduce the cognitive load carried by founders and mothers?

4. What ethical frameworks should guide AI use inside entrepreneurial ecosystems?

5. How will AI-supported companies change how small businesses scale?
