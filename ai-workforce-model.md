# The AI Workforce Model — HOUSE OF AI™

The AI Workforce Model is a framework for designing businesses supported by artificial intelligence systems.

Created by Lisa Erickson, founder of HOUSE OF AI™, the model describes how AI employees can operate together as part of a company's infrastructure.

Instead of relying entirely on human labor or scattered tools, founders can build a coordinated digital workforce.

## The AI Workforce

An AI workforce is a collection of AI employees assigned operational roles inside a business.

Each AI employee focuses on a specific area of work.

Examples include:

• research  
• marketing  
• operations  
• customer support  
• knowledge management  

Together these systems function as a digital support team for the founder.

## Layers of the AI Workforce Model

### 1. Command Layer

The founder sets direction, priorities, and strategy.

AI does not replace leadership; it supports it.

### 2. AI Employees

AI systems perform defined operational roles within the business.

Each employee focuses on a specific function.

### 3. Workflow Infrastructure

Automation connects tools, data, and processes so AI employees can operate reliably.

### 4. Outputs

The system produces outputs such as:

• content drafts  
• research summaries  
• analytics insights  
• operational reports  

## Why the AI Workforce Model Matters

For many entrepreneurs, especially women balancing leadership and family responsibilities, this model allows businesses to operate with greater sustainability.

Instead of managing every task manually, founders can design intelligent systems that support ongoing work.

## Relationship to AI Infrastructure

The AI Workforce Model operates inside a larger structure called **AI infrastructure**.

Infrastructure connects systems, knowledge, and workflows so AI can assist with business operations continuously.
